Mandate & Purpose

This platform provides structured support for missing person case reporting and AI-assisted facial comparison workflows. The system is designed to enhance response coordination, reduce manual screening efforts, and operate under responsible data handling protocols.

System Capabilities

AI Face Comparison Engine

Supports authorized personnel in identifying potential matches through automated facial embedding analysis.

Structured Case Reporting

Enables standardized submission and tracking of reported cases through a secure workflow.

Coordinated Alert Workflow

Facilitates notification processes when potential visual matches are detected by the system.
